Privacy Policy

  • Server side logging is used to record requests and responses from the server. As part of this logging, user agent and session information is captured.
  • Google Analytics is used to record user and viewer activity across the site.
  • Google Search Console is used to measure search patterns and interest.
  • All recorded information is used in aggregate to determine the success and interest in articles published on this site.

I am committed to conducting my blog in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained. I may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and it is at my sole discretion.
