Who is Henry Lawson?

I'm a Software Consultant with ThoughtWorks. I've had the privilege of consulting for clients around the world in Australia, India, UK, USA, Brazil and Singapore. I have a broad set of skills and interests, from Programming Languages (C#, Ruby, Java, JavaScript, Python and Clojure) to Business Management. As I often struggle to stay on top of all the emerging frameworks, tools and trends, I try and keep a focus on principles, patterns and established theories because as the frameworks and tools fade, the patterns, principles and established theories remain.
